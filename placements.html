OVERVIEW

We take pleasure in introducing ourselves as one of the leading private Engineering and Management colleges accredited under MAKAUT & affiliated to AICTE. In line with the visions of the Techno India Group, we at Meghnad Saha Institute of Technology, endeavor to provide a thorough and meaningful education to our students enabling them to meet the exacting requirements of the industries. Indeed, our previous fourteen batches of students have done very well at Campus selections conducted by major Companies. Presently, the college offers B.Tech degree in six disciplines, namely Computer Science Engineering (CSE), Information Technology (IT), Electronics & Communication (ECE), Electrical Engineering (EE), Civil Engineering (CE) & Mechanical Engineering (ME). We also offer BCA, BBA among other UG Courses, and MBA (with four specializations like Marketing, Finance, HR and Systems), M.Tech (Geo-tech, Computer Science & ECE) & MCA degree among PG courses. The goal of Training & Placement Cell is to provide employment opportunities and world class training to our Students in Leading Organizations. We have introduced modern measures towards an all round development of the students as individuals with skills in communication, presentation, group discussion and lateral thinking to ensure their compatibility as international citizens.
